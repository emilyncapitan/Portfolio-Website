## Project Description

Born from the intersection of business strategy and technical craft, this project captures our journey of learning to build together in the same way professional teams do. By trading the classroom for a live, collaborative workspace on GitHub, we moved beyond theory to experience the pulse of a modern development pipeline. It’s more than just a coursework requirement; it’s a hands-on look at how we bridge the gap between management and code to turn a shared vision into a working reality.

* Each member created their own branch

* The project leader reviewed and merged contributions

## Purpose of the Activity

This project is where our classroom learning meets the real world. By trading stacks of paperwork for digital tools, we’ve spent our time learning how to turn messy, manual processes into smooth, automated workflows. Using GitHub wasn't just about learning to code—it was about learning how to communicate and build as a team. From navigating the hurdles of merging different ideas to perfecting the art of a shared project, we’ve gained the hands-on experience needed to bridge the gap between traditional business management and the fast-paced tech industry.
